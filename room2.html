ROOM 02

The Mirror Room

"There is no wrong answer — only the one that's actually yours."

What's Inside

Stand in front of a touch-sensor mirror and a binary question appears: "What scares you most? A. Failing / B. Falling behind others." Whichever you choose unlocks a different safe behind the mirror.

THE TWIST

There's no correct answer — the trait you reveal by choosing becomes the Key Item that opens the next room.
