🚪 The Core Room

A psychological interactive game where you escape by finding your own Core under time pressure

50-60 min · Solo or matched teams up to 4 · Difficulty: Easy

🖼️ See Inside the Rooms →

1

The Decluttering Room

The walls are covered in to-do sticky notes for seminars, networking, internship prep, and workouts — and a numeric lock code is hidden somewhere among them. If you try to keep every single task, the code will never line up.

Twist: Only by boldly tearing some sticky notes off the wall do the remaining tasks line up into the lock code.

2

The Mirror Room

Stand in front of a touch-sensor mirror and a binary question appears. "What scares you most? A. Failing / B. Falling behind others." Whichever you choose unlocks a different safe behind the mirror.

Twist: There's no right answer — the trait you reveal by choosing becomes the Key Item that opens the next room.

3

The Core Design Studio

A large timeline board sits at the center of the room. Fill in the blanks using the Key Items and value cards you collected in Rooms 1 and 2. Complete the board to unlock the final door.

Twist: Cards picked just because "everyone else picks them" won't complete the board. Only "the 3 things I can truly commit to" will open the door.
